Computer Science & Engineering (Accredited by NBA - UG : CSE )

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering was established in the year 2007 with an intake of 60 students in B.Tech program and 18 students in M.Tech program in the year 2012. The Department has state of the art infrastructure and computing equipment supported by high speed internet and wireless networks. The Department has been maintaining high standards in imparting quality education in Computer Science. The faculty in the department consists of Six Professors, two Associate Professors and five Assistant Professors (C) out of which Six are completed Ph.D and Two are pursuing Ph.D. Our faculty aims at delivering top class education blending their rich research experience with classroom teaching. The major areas of research include Image Processing, Real time operating Systems, Neural Networks, Data mining, Information retrieval and Web mining, Information Security, Networks, Operating Systems, Distributed Systems, Parallel processing and Human-Computer Interactions. ©

Mechanical Engineering (Accredited by NBA - UG : ME )

The Department of Mechanical Engineering (ME) has started in 2007 with intake of 60 in UG and has been adding features in its cap every year with both curricular and non curricular activities. The department started its PG program in Engineering Design in the year 2011 with an intake of 18 and sponsored seats of 6. The Mechanical Engineering has state of art laboratories such as Engineering workshop, Production Technology, Thermal Engineering, Heat & Mass transfer, Metrology, Machine tools, Metallurgy & Material science, Instrumentation, Fluid Mechanics & Hydraulics Machinery labs and CAD/CAM labs. The Department is having 15 teaching staff, 18 non-teaching staff. The teachers of Mechanical Engineering have written more than 20 text books and have enthusiastic supervision for research. Presently, about 20 scholars are working in various advanced fields of Mechanical Engineering and already produced 4 PhDs. The Mechanical Engineering department is imparting practical knowledge in Automobile, Refrigeration, Air conditioning and other relevant areas under Reverse and Re-Engineering centre. The teachers of the department have published several technical papers in national and inter-national journals and conducting national seminars and conferences.
